1)	Sales Person will generate quotation request in this project. If no item exist, they could have option to Create new item for that description. If that description already exists, system must to give warning, that similar description of item is already available and also show that similar item with description before creating new item.
2)	After that sales person will generate request for quotation in the project, sales person must to enter the expected order quantity.
3)	After request generated. It will give notification + email to the user who will generate BOM (Recipe). In BOM, he will create BOM / KG and select all raw material item which will form BOM / KG including wastage for each process. Like extrusion, printing, lamination, etc.
4)	Then that BOM will go to accountant(with notification + email) who will enter the cost of Raw Materials (he will check from his ERP system) last purchase. Then he will submit. Moreover he can enter landed cost(estimated – by percentage or by value). Also he can add other FOH .Then BOM will be ready for finished goods quotation(which sales person requested). After all BOM ready under quotation request. Then accountant will process this request to Managing director where managing director will generate quotation by entering the sales price. He could able to see bill of material included landed cost and other FOH. Moreover he can see the profit margin after entering the sales price.Also he can see Material cost % and other costs %(including landed cost + other FOH). After he approve the quotation. It will give notification to sales person with email also from where he can download the Sales Quotation what he requested.
